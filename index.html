3. Census

In this assignment, I'm looking to visualise a few interesting characteristics pertaining to the Asian demographics in America, mainly, sub-ethnic diversity and clustering in and around "Gateway cities". Click here!


Week 1: Research, Concepts, Sketches

Explore what is available as census data here. Come up with three “stories” you are interested in. Create a name, description and sketch for each and and find visual references. Submit one presentation on Stellar either as pdf or Google slides. Click here to see my sketches!


Week 2: Parsing & First design iteration

Start working with the API and javascript / P5. Can you get the data you need? Is it available at all? Think about how you would go about parsing, cleaning and structuring the data you get.
In parallel, start working on a design for your visualization. Consider layout, typography, visualization and interface elements. Consider designing for desktop and mobile.


Week 3: Prototype & Final design

Create a working prototype of your visualization. This should be your “minimum viable product”. All pieces should be in place, your visualization strategy clear. Also create a “pixel perfect” visual design that defines all aspects in full detail.


Week 4: Refinement and final working app

Bring all components together in one final developed version of your visualization. Consider responsive behavior, filtering, states and animation.
